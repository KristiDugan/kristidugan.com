About

Kristi Dugan has been a Front-End Web Developer since the early days of CSS. She has helped companies small and large expand their web presence in the areas of arts education, online learning and healthcare. With an enthusiasm for researching and utilizing web technologies, she excels at solving the problem at hand in the most fitting manner for both the client and end user. Her background in the arts, language, and business has aided her career in technology by giving her an appreciation for good design and the ability to communicate effectively with stakeholders of varying levels of technical expertise. Kristi is currently working with a group of women in the Moms Can Code School, which helps mothers gain the skills necessary to work remotely while still being there for their family. When not studying to become a Full-Stack JavaScript Developer, she enjoys spending time with her husband and three young children. Find out more about Kristi.
